Campus Dashboard Features:

1. Campus Announcements Feed: Admin posts important updates like events, exams, holiday notices. Students can view announcements sorted by categories and dates.

2. Lost & Found Section: Students report or search for lost/found items with item images and locations. Smart filters based on item category and date.

3. Mini Timetable Scheduler: Students can input and manage their weekly class schedule. Timetable displayed in calendar/grid format with edit/delete options.

4. Hostel Complaint Registration: Raise complaints (water, electricity, cleaning, etc.) and track complaint status: pending, in-progress, resolved (admin-controlled).

5. User Authentication System: Student and admin login/signup with role-based access. JWT or Firebase-based secure session management.

6. Skill Exchange Marketplace: Students list skills they can teach (e.g., coding, design, languages). Others can book short peer learning sessions. Promotes collaborative upskilling within campus.

7. Tech News & Opportunities Feed: Curated section for hackathons, internships, tech news. Keeps students updated and competitive.
